Superior Home Automation Technology

The Surreal Smart Home is an affordable home automation system that is simple to setup, is extremely expandable, and allows you to control anything from anywhere.

Our modules easily attach directly to existing light switches or outlet plugs and do not require modifications to existing electrical systems. Our intuitive user interface enables automation by creation of simple rules, actions, and alerts. Our application allows you to remotely view or change the status of lights, appliances, locks and more from anywhere.

At surreal industries we focus on safety, reliability, and expansion. Every module is completely solid-state and has internal safeguards to keep your home and family safe. Modules are individually capable of accurately regulating power including dimming and preventing overloads, transmitting information about the temperature of rooms, monitoring power usage and their current status on the network. Our modules are completely wireless and can communicate over very long distances and self-associate when more modules are added to the network.

Our modules and applications are targeted towards housing developers, contractors and retail applications. The installation of our modules into new or existing homes significantly increase a home"s safety, market value, increases usability, quality and saves power.
